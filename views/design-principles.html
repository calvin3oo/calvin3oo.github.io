Hick's law

Google

Google represents Hick's law well because there is almost nothing on the opening page to look at. There is a search box, and a small amount of menu options on the top right corner of the page.

White Space and Clean Design

Github

Github represents white space and clean design well because it spaces all of it's elements out very well, and although you could do a thousand things with github, there are only gives a few options on the main screen. The bottom of the screenshot also demonstrates how they have a clear simple description for starting with something in github, and they have a very clear button right below the description to get started.

Fitt's Law

Youtube

Youtube represents Fitt's law well because of the size of everything. Two youtube videos take up the whole mobile screen, taking up more space, making it easier to click on a video that I might be interested in.
